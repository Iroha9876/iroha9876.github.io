Content Policy for Galleries, Text Posts, Comments, Chatrooms, and Direct Messages

1. Overview

Our platform supports diverse creative and social expression through galleries, text posts, and interactive features such as comments, chatrooms, and direct messages. To maintain a safe, respectful, and legally compliant environment for all users, the following content policies apply.

2. General Content Rules

  • All content must comply with Japanese laws and our Terms of Service.
  • Content that is illegal, harmful, abusive, discriminatory, or invades privacy is strictly prohibited.
  • Users are responsible for the content they post or share.
  • We promote freedom of artistic and personal expression within legal and ethical boundaries.

3. Sensitive Content and Flagging

  • Content containing nudity, sexual themes, violence, or other mature material must be marked with the Sensitive Content flag.
  • This includes artworks, photos, text descriptions, and media that may not be suitable for all audiences.
  • Users must accurately flag sensitive content; failure to do so may result in content removal or account penalties.
  • Viewers will be given a clear warning and choice to reveal sensitive content.

4. Galleries and Artworks

  • NSFW / R18 artworks are allowed when properly flagged, provided they:
    • Depict legal and consensual adult themes.
    • Do not depict minors or characters that can reasonably be interpreted as minors in sexual contexts.
    • Avoid content involving non-consensual acts, exploitation, or illegal behaviors.
  • Partial censoring (blurring, props) is acceptable but does not exempt content from flagging.
  • Artworks depicting fantasy or aged-up versions of characters originally minors are allowed if clearly indicated.
  • Artworks with nudity for non-erotic, artistic purposes (e.g., classical sculpture) are allowed under sensitive flagging.
  • Content involving illegal acts, voyeurism, or revenge porn is prohibited.

5. Text Posts

  • Text posts may include mature themes but must not contain:
    • Hate speech, threats, or incitement of violence.
    • Glorification or promotion of illegal activities.
    • Non-consensual sexual content or explicit descriptions involving minors.
  • Sensitive topics (e.g., drug use, self-harm) are allowed for expressive or help-seeking purposes if presented responsibly and flagged appropriately.
  • Users are encouraged to use trigger warnings or content notes for potentially distressing content.

6. Comments, Chatrooms, and Direct Messages

  • All communication must respect community standards of respect and consent.
  • Harassment, bullying, threats, or unsolicited explicit content are prohibited.
  • Sexual content in chats or messages must only occur between consenting users and respect platform rules.
  • Users should report any abuse or violations to platform moderators.
  • The platform may monitor or moderate chats to enforce policies and legal compliance, respecting privacy as much as possible.

7. Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual exploitation or pornography (including fictional or drawn content depicting minors sexually) is strictly illegal and banned.
  • Content depicting non-consensual acts, revenge porn, voyeurism, or privacy violations.
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or content inciting violence.
  • Content promoting or glorifying terrorism, self-harm, or suicide without responsible context.
  • Spam, scams, malware, or other harmful technical content.

8. User Responsibilities

  • Flag sensitive content accurately and promptly.
  • Respect other users and their boundaries.
  • Report any illegal or harmful content or behavior.
  • Understand that content flagged or reported may be reviewed and removed.

9. Enforcement and Appeals

  • Violation of this policy can result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans.
  • Serious illegal violations may be reported to law enforcement authorities.
  • Users may appeal moderation decisions through e-mail.

10. Age Restrictions and Verification

  • Users must be 18 years or older to post or view NSFW/R18 content.
  • Age verification may be implemented.
  • Users under 18 are prohibited from accessing adult content.

11. Policy Updates

  • This policy may be updated to reflect changes in laws, technology, or community standards.
  • Users will be notified of significant changes.
