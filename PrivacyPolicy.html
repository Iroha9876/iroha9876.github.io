Privacy Policy

  1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when you use our application ("Chat2").

  1. Information We Collect

Mandatory information collected during registration:

  • Username
  • Password hash (securely stored)
  • Registration date
  • Unique User Identification Number (UIN) generated by Chat2 for internal use only

Optional information you provide:

  • Profile picture
  • Bio
  • Posts, comments, galleries, chat logs, and other user-generated content
  1. How We Use Your Information
  • To authenticate and manage your account securely.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve Chat2’s features and services.
  • To enable your social interactions and content sharing within the platform.

Important: We do not use your optional profile or content data for commercial purposes, telemetry, AI training, advertising, or selling to third parties.

  1. Data Sharing and Disclosure
  • We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data or user-generated content to third parties.
  • Disclosure of your information will only occur:
    • When required by law or legal process.
    • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Chat2, its users, or the public.
  1. Data Security
  • We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including storing passwords as secure hashes.
  • While we strive to protect your data, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
  1. Data Retention
  • We retain your personal information and User Content as long as your account is active or as necessary to comply with legal obligations.
  • Upon account deletion, your data will be removed from active systems, though backups may retain some information temporarily.
  1. Your Rights
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data through Chat2.
  1. Children’s Privacy
  • Chat2 is intended for users aged 13 and above.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
  1. International Data Transfers
  • As Chat2 operates in Japan, your data is stored and processed primarily within Japan.
  1. Changes to This Privacy Policy
  • We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
  • Updates will be posted within Chat2, and significant changes will be communicated to users.
  1. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our support team via Chat2.
